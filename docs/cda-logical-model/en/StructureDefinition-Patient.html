Official URL: http://hl7.org/cda/stds/core/StructureDefinition/Patient Version: 2.0.1-sd-202510-matchbox-patch
Active as of 2025-10-29 Computable Name: Patient

A LivingSubject as a recipient of health care services from a healthcare provider. The patient is the player; the provider is the scoper.
