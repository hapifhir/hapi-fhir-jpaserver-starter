Data Type Profile: ts: Point in Time

Official URL: http://hl7.org/cda/stds/core/StructureDefinition/ts-simple Version: 2.0.0-sd-202406-matchbox-patch
Active as of 2024-06-19 Computable Name: ts

A quantity specifying a point on the axis of natural time. A point in time is most often represented as a calendar expression.

Usage:

  • Use this PrimitiveType Constraint: TS: PointInTime (V3 Data Type)
